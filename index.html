Phase 1: Launch & Learning ($2,500/mo)

Budget Allocation

Google Ads 60%
Meta Ads 25%
Testing 10%
Retargeting 5%

Key Channels & Focus

Google Search Ads

Focus on Brand, Generic, and Competitor terms to establish a CPL baseline. (Est. CPL: $100-$125)

Meta Ads (FB/IG)

Test "Owner-Operator" vs. "Semi-Absent" personas with "Escape the 9-5" creatives. (Est. CPL: $60-$100)

Phase 1: Testing & Retargeting

Testing Priorities ($250)

  • Month 1: LinkedIn Ads pilot & YouTube Pre-Roll test.
  • Month 2: Reddit Ads exploration & new Meta formats.
  • Month 3: TikTok (for younger owners) & Google Display.
  • Success Criteria: Achieve <$150 CPL with a clear path to scale.

Retargeting Segments ($125)

  • Landing Page Visitors (7-day): Show testimonials, trust signals.
  • Content Downloaders (30-day): Show Discovery Day invites, ROI data.
  • Video Viewers (14-day): Show case studies, next steps.
  • Expected CPL: $40 - $60 (warmed audience).

Phase 2: Optimization ($3,500/mo)

Budget Allocation

Risk Mitigation & Contingency

Platform Diversification Rules

  • Max Concentration: No single channel > 60% of budget.
  • Min Channels: Minimum 3 active channels at all times.
  • Testing: New channels tested at 5-10% before scaling.

Performance Triggers

  • Scale Up: CPL 20%+ below target.
  • Scale Down: CPL 30%+ above target (60+ days).
  • Kill Switch: CPL 50%+ above target (90+ days).

Conclusion & Q&A

Channel Balancing

  • Proven Channels (Google, Meta): Consistent lead flow.
  • Premium Channels (LinkedIn): High-value prospects.
  • Supporting Channels (Retargeting): Conversion optimization.
  • Future Channels (SEO): Long-term cost reduction.

Resource Balancing

  • 70% Execution: Hands-on campaign management.
  • 20% Strategy: Optimization & analysis.
  • 10% Innovation: Testing & experimentation.
